About Me!

LeBron James

LeBron James, often referred to as "King James," is widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time. Born on December 30, 1984, in Akron, Ohio, he rose to fame as a high school basketball prodigy before being selected as the first overall pick in the 2003 NBA Draft by the Cleveland Cavaliers. Over his illustrious career, LeBron has played for the Miami Heat, Cleveland Cavaliers, and Los Angeles Lakers, winning four NBA championships and earning numerous accolades, including four MVP awards. Known for his versatility, athleticism, and basketball IQ, he is the NBA's all-time leading scorer and has made significant contributions both on and off the court, including philanthropy and advocacy for social justice. His impact on the sport and culture is unparalleled, solidifying his legacy as a global icon.

Bronny James Jr.

Bronny James, born on October 6, 2004, in Akron, Ohio, is the eldest son of NBA superstar LeBron James. Following in his father's footsteps, Bronny has made a name for himself in basketball, showcasing his talent as a skilled guard. He attended Sierra Canyon School in Los Angeles, where he gained national attention for his impressive performances and was named a McDonald's All-American in 2023. Bronny later played college basketball for the USC Trojans before being drafted by the Los Angeles Lakers in 2024, making history as part of the first active father-son duo in the NBA. Known for his dedication, athleticism, and basketball IQ, Bronny continues to build his legacy while carrying the weight of high expectations, and is in the NBA in the lakers with his father

Bryce James Jr.

Bryce James, born on June 14, 2007, in Cuyahoga Falls, Ohio, is the youngest son of NBA legend LeBron James and Savannah James. Standing at 6'6", Bryce is known for his impressive basketball skills and is considered one of the most promising young players in his age group. He currently attends Sierra Canyon School in California, where he plays as a shooting guard and has gained attention for his sharp shooting and court vision. Bryce has already received college basketball offers and is steadily building his reputation as a talented athlete, following in the footsteps of his father and older brother, Bronny James. With his dedication and potential, Bryce is poised to make a significant impact in the basketball world.
